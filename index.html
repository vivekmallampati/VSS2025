Vishwa Sangh Shibir 2025

Global Gathering of Swayamsevaks

Shri Vishwa Niketan is privileged to organize the Vishwa Sangh Shibir 2025, an international gathering of Pravasi Bharatiyas and delegates from various countries. The event will bring together representatives from over 70 countries to celebrate and strengthen the global Hindu fraternity.

The Shibir aims to foster cultural exchange, national pride, and collective action for global well-being rooted in the timeless values of Sanatan Dharma.

About Vishwa Sangh Shibir 2025

Our Mission

The Vishwa Sangh Shibir 2025 represents a historic gathering that transcends geographical boundaries to unite the global Hindu community. This five-day event serves as a platform for cultural exchange, spiritual growth, and collective action rooted in the timeless principles of Sanatan Dharma.

Event Highlights

  • Cultural performances and exhibitions from 70+ countries
  • Spiritual discourses and meditation sessions
  • Workshops on Hindu philosophy and practices
  • Community service initiatives
  • Networking opportunities for global collaboration

Core Values

Our gathering is built upon the foundation of Dharma - the eternal principles that guide righteous living. Through this event, we aim to strengthen the bonds of the global Hindu fraternity while contributing to the well-being of humanity at large.

Accommodation

    A modest dormitory accommodation (separate for swayamsevaks and sevikas) with common wash areas will be arranged during the shibir.

    Accommodation during the post-shibir tour to Srisailam and Yadadri will be arranged as per the destination.

    Pre and post shibir accommodation will be arranged in Bhagyanagar.

Travel

Rail reservations in Bharat start 60 days in advance. It is advisable to finalize the plans and make bookings for the return journey before coming for VSS.

Please note that the check-in baggage limit for domestic flights in Bharat is 15 kgs.

If any train ticket booking needs to be done by the shibir vyavastha, please convey the travel details to Santosh Pillai (santovpillai@gmail.com). Please mention name as on passport, gender and age for such bookings.

Pickup

Arrangements for pickup will be made at Bhagyanagar airport, railway station and bus terminals.
